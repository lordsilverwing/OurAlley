About Our Alley

Origin

It started with an idea for a website that would connect neighbors together, to increase community building. The best way to connect with others and to foster those relationships is to find a mutual love for something. We decided dogs would be this entry point as dogs are pretty awesome. That then led to the idea of establishing playdates for those dogs. Cast away the frivolity of human society, embrace dog society! Build healthy dog communities where are four legged best friends can thrive!

What is OurAlley?

Simply put, it is a place where you can see dogs in your area and set up playdates with them for your dog. Either set up your own playdates or receive ones from others. You can also see details about each dog so you can find the perfect match for your best friend!

A Dog Community

Dogs and humans are not so different when it comes to socialization. Sure we may be fancier and more sophisticated, however, the basics remain the same. That is what we want to foster for our companions, a place that you can arrange for them to have a good time. Hopefully your dogs will love it and by extension improve your overall quality of life at home!

Should I use OurAlley?

Personally I have only one dog and he yearns to socialize with other furry four-legged beings. We bipedal beings just get boring sometimes. If you are in the same boat as me or have multiple dogs who just need to spice things up then I recommend you sign up today. Do it for them.
